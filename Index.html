Bike Sharing Analytics

A comprehensive case study on a fictional company of Bike Rental products. The Purpose of this case study is to analyze and come up with a detailed solution to an underwhelming Demand of the Bikes in the Rental Company.Also to build a regression model using Linear Regression to predict the future Demand of the bike rental company.

Problem Statement

A Bike Sharing system is a service in which bike are made availaible for shared use to individuals on a short term basis for a price or free. Many bike share systems allow people to borrow a bike from a dock which is usually computer controlled where in the user enters the payment information , and the system unlocks it. The business has recently suffered a dip in the revenue due to the ongoing pandemic. Our job is to perform thorough analysis and build a regression model further which predicts the demand and enable the organisation to make business priority decisons.

Methodology and Environment Used

We will start with an intital data prepration by inspecting the dataframe and doing the appropriate cleaning and feature transformation. This is further followed by the Regular EDA and statistical Analysis on the independent features. This will be further aided by building the model by using the most appropriate feature selection techniques to curb the effects of multicollinearity.
